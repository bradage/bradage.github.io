My site name will be weather update.

Weatherupdate.com provides accurate and to-the-minute weather updates so our clients can plan for their outdoor adventure. Whether they are hiking, planting, harvesting or sporting events weatherupdate.com is committed to keeping our area updated with the most accurate weather conditions and forecasts. Clients will be able to see hourly forecasts to 14 day forecasts to help them plan their outdoor activity. We also host a community calendar to help keep the community informed on activities available in their area. For clients that enjoy the night sky, we host a night sky calendar for determining optimal sky watching nights or great nights for activities under a bright moon.
